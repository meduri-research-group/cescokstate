Research

Our Research Quest

The lab advances electrochemistry, functional materials, and catalysis to address energy conversion, storage, and water sustainability. Below are the core thrust areas (mirroring the Quest section on the home page).

Themes

The Quest Areas

Hover each card to see a short description.

01

Advanced Functional Materials

Design and develop innovative multi-functional material architectures based on non-noble metals and metal compounds.

02

Solar Fuels

Photo/electrochemical water splitting (H₂), H₂O₂ production, and CO₂ conversion to value-added products.

03

Energy Storage

Metal-ion batteries, Li-S/Se batteries, metal-air batteries, and in-situ mechanistic studies.

04

Water Purification

Photo/electrocatalytic organic waste degradation and contaminant removal.

05

Catalysis for Agriculture

Cleaner energy routes and improved efficiency for ammonia/fertilizer technologies.

06

Smart Devices

Portable photo/electrocatalyst-based energy generation and disinfection systems.
